Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I place an order?
Click "Order Now" on any product to fill out our order form. Your details will be sent to us via WhatsApp, and we'll confirm availability, pricing, and delivery with you directly.
Do you offer custom designs?
Yes! We specialize in custom designs tailored to your preferences. Contact us with your ideas, and we'll create something unique for you.
What are your delivery options?
We offer delivery across Accra and surrounding areas. Delivery typically takes 3-7 business days depending on your location. We can also arrange for pickup at our showroom.
How long does it take to make a custom garment?
Custom orders typically take 2-4 weeks depending on the complexity of the design and our current workload. We'll give you an exact timeline when you place your order.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Mobile Money (MTN, Vodafone, AirtelTigo), bank transfers, and cash payments. Payment details will be provided when we confirm your order.
Can I return or exchange an item?
We want you to be completely satisfied! If there's an issue with sizing or quality, contact us within 7 days and we'll work out an exchange or alteration.
Do you have a physical store I can visit?
Yes! We have a showroom in Achimota, Accra. However, we operate by appointment only to give you our full attention. Contact us to schedule a visit.
What sizes do you offer?
We offer sizes from Small to XXL for our ready-made pieces. For custom orders, we can create any size. Check our Size Guide for measurements.

Still Have Questions?

We're here to help! Contact us directly.
